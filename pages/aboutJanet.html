Meet Janet

~ Psychiatric Nurse Practitioner, PMHNP, APRN, MSN

Worried that your medication is effecting your mental wellness? Let's unlock your path to mental well-being. I am an experienced psychiatric nurse practitioner who can provide you compassionate care, personalized treatment plans, through a mind-body approach. I provide medication management services to individuals of all ages. I see patients who suffer from anxiety, depression, PTSD, bipolar disorder, substance and/or alcohol use disorder, which includes medication assisted treatment for opiate use disorder. While mental health medications can seem confusing, I can clarify things for you. Allow me to help you work out an effective medication management treatment plan.

~ Janet Damiano
